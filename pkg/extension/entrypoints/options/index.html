Privacy Kudos

Tab grouping

Tabs group only when opened with a _tab_group marker (the CLI adds it). The name comes from that marker; pick the color here.

Demo narration — video mode

Voice for /dg:demo video recordings, spoken locally by Kokoro TTS. The model downloads once (~86 MB) and is cached.

Test narration

Loads Kokoro and generates a sample with the selected voice — a quick check that local TTS works here. Preview it, then download the .webm.

Privacy

Everything runs on your machine. No accounts, no keys, no tracking.

  • No telemetry. The extension sends no analytics, tracking, or usage data — ever.
  • Narration is local. Kokoro TTS runs in your browser; your text and audio never leave your machine.
  • Recordings stay local. Demo videos save straight to your Downloads; nothing is uploaded.
  • Only network use: a one-time download of the open-source voice model from Hugging Face, then cached offline.
  • Settings stay on-device in your browser's extension storage. No cloud sync of your data by us.
